A SIPOC diagram is used in process improvement to represent the
different components of a process. The acronym stands for Suppliers,
Inputs, Process, Outputs, and Customers.

*SIPOC diagrams are best for:* Providing a high-level view of a process
which helps visualize the sequence of events and their interconnections.
